About Us

"Eagle Wood" is a company that sells, buys logs of various types and processes them. Main activity is production of palletized firewood. The company has been operating under that name since 2020, and it has existed for 20 years, when we started as a family business, which was later registered as "Eagle Wood". Our mission is to offer our customers a product that, we can guarantee, meets all the requirements in terms of product quality with affordable prices.

We offer:

  • Quality firewood;
  • Compliance with customer requirements and delivery deadlines;
  • Flexibility in meeting customer requirements in terms of processing and packaging of products;
